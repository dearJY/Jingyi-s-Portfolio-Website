Hobbyist

        an app simplifies&enhances movie watching experience

Design Process

needfinding

Observing and Interviewing people are very good ways to find design oppotunities. Our interviewers told us that they recorded a lot of movies(books, music etc.) that they wanted to watch or review in their memo but finally lost motivation to watch them or even forget about them as the time past by. What a pity people missed those movies they were interested in. We decided to design an app to help users take use of movies they collected.

storyboards

What problems that our design can solve? Thinking about some real scenarios and drawing the storyboards, we found that our design could help users make a satisfying decision on what movies to watch when nothing came up in their minds.

prototype&heuristic evaluation feedback

we started to think about what features could realize our goals and draw paper prototype based on them.

Feature 1. users could search moviesï¼Œ classify and save them into movie lists that they have watched("history") or were going to watch("watchlist");

Feature 2. to take use of movies in these two "repositories", we provided users with features of randomly choosing a movie from "watchlist" or recommending a movie to other users from "history".

Asking friends to operate paper prototype and claasifying problems using beruistic evaluation was quite helpful for us to revise our design.

Wireframe

Using Adobe Comp, Adobe Photoshop and Invision to create our app wireframe. It was a good chance to design and revise the layout of the app on wireframe. We aimed to let users use each features in as few steps as possible.

Implementation

Start to make the app real!

Implement the app using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Jquery and NodeJS.

URL:hobbyists.herokuapp.com

testing

We asked some friends to use our app in front of us and give us advice(In-person Tests). According to the advice they gave, we used "google analytics" to do A/B Tests for some features, and analyzed the data based on chi-squared statitics. These testing results helped us optimize the layout, functionalities and asthetic details of our app.
