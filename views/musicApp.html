music app - wireframe

      a music app with optmized layout and features

Design Process

Interviews

What kind of music app do users really want? Interviewing music apps users and observing how they use music apps is the most direct way to find users' needs. Our group interviewed 9 people who regularly liten to music through their mobile phones. They showed us what they liked and disliked in different user scenarios.

Comparisons

Analyzing results of all the interviews, we classified all the user scenarios into three groups - "Create Playlist", "Manage Playlist" and "other activities". We chose several music apps including Spotify, SoundCloud, Google Play Music, Microsoft Groove, Apple Music and Deezer to compare their functionalities in three scenario groups above. During this process we got to know strength and weakness of many music apps, which were important resource of our redesign.

Competitive analysis

Main page and Genres are essential parts of a music app because users use them most often. We chose three apps(Spotify, SoundCloud and Google Play Music) and drew the hierarchical sitemaps of their main pages and genres. In this way, we knew how different layouts work and what aspects can be optimized.

Wireframe

According to the results of interviews, comparisons and competitive analysis, we sketched out each page of our music app. Using the sketch to going through workflow of each user scenario helped us revise our design. Based on the sketch, the wireframe was finally made by using Adobe Comp, Adobe Photoshop and Invision.
